Things to Eat

These are some of our favourite restaurants spread throughout London.

Gourmet Burger Kitchen - GBK

GBK is a great burger restaurant with amazing food (& fries). There are lots of GBK restaurants spread throughout London including, St. Paul's & Waterloo. The GBK in St Paul's is very close to London Blackfriars Station, from there you can take the Circle/District Line to London Victoria or take the mainline trains to London St. Pancras International & Brighton via London Bridge. The GBK in Waterloo is very close to London Waterloo Station with London Underground Routes to Euston, Baker Street, Bank (This route has been closed during COVID) & Oxford Circus.

Borough Market

Borough Market is a large market in Central London. There are many market stands and restaurants in the market. One of my favourite restaurants is El Pastor. Borough Market is very close to London Bridge with trains to St. Pancras and London Underground services to Moorgate (to get to Liverpool Street).

Tandoori Nights

Tandoori Nights is an Indian restuarant with amazing flavours. Tandoori Nights is in East Dulwich with regular trains to London Bridge and East Croydon.

Monkatsu

Monkatsu is a really good Japanese restaurant. They have sushi and many other things. Monkatsu is also very close to East Dulwich Station.

Polpo

Polpo is an award-winning Venetian Restaurant. There are 2 locations: Soho & Chelsea. Polpo in Soho is very close to Oxford Circus Underground Station, from there you can take the Victoria Line to Victoria and Kings Cross St. Pancras, you can also take the Bakerloo Line to Waterloo and Baker Street or take the Central Line to Liverpool Street and Shepherd's Bush.
