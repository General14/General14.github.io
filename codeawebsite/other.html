Things you can do to make more advanced and interesting websites

HTML is made up of elements. They are sometimes knows as tags. These are the things that you types for example the paragraph element. Here are some of the most important.

H1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6

The h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6 tag is what you use for headings. These tags are very important to your first steps as a website maker.

p

The p tag is what you use to make paragraphs. This is a very important tag as it shows most of the things on your page.

div

The div tag can be used to define sections in a document. This can be useful with CSS.

b, i, u, s

The b tag makes text bold. The i tag stands for emphasis which makes the text italic. The u tag makes you text underlined. The s tag makes the text have a strikethrough.

nav

The nav tag can be used to make a navigation bar. You can find out more in the Other/Advanced tab.

sup, sub

The sup tag stands for superscript, this makes the text smaller and at the top of the line. The sub stands for subscript, this makes the text smaller and at the bottom of the line.

Lists

li

The li tag stands for list item. This is very important because it is what you use to make items in you list.

ul

The ul tag stands for unordered list. It is very important and it is what makes up most lists.

dl

The dl tag stands for detailed list. You can use this to provide more information under your list items.

ol

The ol tag stands for ordered list. This is what you use to make lists that start with 1.
