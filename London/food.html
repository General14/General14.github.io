Things to Eat

London is full of so many great things to eat. Here you can find restaurants, cafés, and snacks.

Restaurants:

There are lots of restaurants in London. Here is a list of my favourites.

Monkatsu
Superb sushi and other Japanese food. Located at 49 N Cross Rd, London SE22 9ET.
Mamma Dough
Delicious pizza and other Italian food. There are lots of locations. Here is a list.
Tandoori Nights
Tasty Indian food full of flavour. Located at 73 Lordship Ln, London SE22 8EP.
Chisou - Mayfair
Sushi in the heart of London, just like being in Japan. On the pricey side but worth it. Located at 22-23 Woodstock St, London W1C 2AR.
Plum Valley
Amazing Cantonese food and dim sum. Located at 20 Gerrard St, London W1D 6JQ.
Wuli Wuli
Excellent Chinese food. Located at 15 Camberwell Church St, London SE5 8TR.
Momo Sushi
Great sushi and other Japanese food. Located at 23 Camberwell Church St, London SE5 8TR.
Silk Road
Authentic Chinese food in south east London. Located at 49 Camberwell Church St, London SE5 8TR.

Pubs:

The Herne Tavern
Delectable pub food and great drinks. Located at 2 Forest Hill Rd, London SE22 0RR.
The Lordship Pub
Yummy pub food and good drinks. Located at 211 Lordship Ln, London SE22 8HA.

Cafés:

There are also lots of cafés in London. Here are some of my favourites.

French Café Bistro
Great coffee and other French food. There are also lots of French products here. Located at 44 Forest Hill Rd, London SE22 0RR.
St David Coffee House / Bon Dulwich / Browns of Brockley
Really tasty coffee and pastries, as well as great local bread. There are locations in Forest Hill (St David Coffee House, 5 David's Rd, London SE23 3EP), East Dulwich (Bon, 43 Lordship Ln, London SE22 8EW), and Brockley (Browns of Brockley, 5-6 Coulgate St, London SE4 2RW).
Gail's Bakery
Delicious pastries and bread, good coffee. They also sell great yoghurt, milk, and butter. Here is a list of all the locations.
Norris + Knight
Delectable Spanish food and amazing coffee. Located at 118 Forest Hill Rd, London SE22 0RS.

Snacks:

In London, there are lots of places to have a quick snack when you are visiting something.

Cuppacha
Excellent bubble tea in Chinatown. Located at 23 Newport Ct, London WC2H 7JS.
Bubblewrap
Exceptional bubble waffles with a wide range of flavours in Chinatown. Located at 24 Wardour St, London W1D 6QJ.
Chez Lindsay
Great crêpes, but a bit far away. Located at 11 Hill Rise, Richmond TW10 6UQ. If you're going here why not go to Kew Gardens.
Chin Chin Labs
Amazing ice cream made with liquid nitrogen. Located in Camden Market (49-50 Camden Lock Pl, London NW1 8AF) Soho (54 Greek St, London W1D 3DS).
