Things to Eat

London is full of so many amazing things to eat. Here you can find restaurants, cafés, and snacks.

Restaurants:

There are lots of restaurants in London, here is a list of my favourites.

Monkatsu
Amazing sushi and other Japanese food. Located at 49 N Cross Rd, London SE22 9ET.
Mamma Dough
Delicious pizza and other Italian food. There are lots of locations. Here is a list.
Tandoori Nights
Tasty Indian food full of amazing flavour. Located at 73 Lordship Ln, London SE22 8EP.
Chisou - Mayfair
Sushi in the heart of London. Located at 22-23 Woodstock St, London W1C 2AR.
Plum Valley
Amazing Cantonese food and dim sum. Located at 20 Gerrard St, London W1D 6JQ.
Wuli Wuli
Delicious Chinese food with a Japanese twist. Located at 15 Camberwell Church St, London SE5 8TR.
Momo Sushi
Amazing sushi and other great Japanese food. Located at 23 Camberwell Church St, London SE5 8TR.

Cafés:

There are also lots of cafés in London. Here are some of my favourites.

French Café Bistro
Great coffee and other French food. There are also lots of French products here. Located at 44 Forest Hill Rd, London SE22 0RR.
St David Coffee House / Bon Dulwich / Browns of Brockley
Really great coffee and pastries, as well as great local bread. There are locations in Forest Hill (St David Coffee House) (5 David's Rd, London SE23 3EP), East Dulwich (Bon) (43 Lordship Ln, London SE22 8EW), and Brockley (Browns of Brockley) (5-6 Coulgate St, London SE4 2RW).
Gail's Bakery
Delicious pastries and bread. They also sell great yoghurt, milk, and butter. Here is a list of all the locations.

Snacks:

In London, there are lots of places to have a quick snack when you are visiting something.

Cuppacha - Chinatown
Excellent bubble tea. Located at 23 Newport Ct, London WC2H 7JS.
Bubblewrap - London
Exceptional bubble waffles with a wide range of flavours. Located at 24 Wardour St, London W1D 6QJ.
Chez Lindsay
Great crêpes from a delicious place, but it is a bit far away. Located at 11 Hill Rise, Richmond TW10 6UQ. If you're going here why not go to Kew Gardens.
