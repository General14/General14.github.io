Things to Eat

London is full of so many amazing things to eat. Here you can find restaurants, cafés, and snacks.

Restaurants:

There are lots of restaurants in London, here is a list of my favourites.

Monkatsu

Amazing sushi and other Japanese food. Located in the suburb of East Dulwich.

Mamma Dough - Honor Oak Park

Delicious pizza and other Italian food. Located in Honor Oak Park. There are also many other locations.

Tandoori Nights

Tasty Indian food full of amazing flavour. Also located in East Dulwich.

Chisou - Mayfair

Sushi and Izakaya in the heart of London. Located in Mayfair, there is also another location in Knightsbridge.

Cafés:

There are also lots of cafés in London. Here are some of my favourites.

French Café Bistro

Great coffee and other French food. They are also lots of French products here. Located in East Dulwich, near Peckham Rye Park.

Bon - Dulwich

Really good coffee and pastries, as well as great local bread. Located in East Dulwich, there are also locations in Forest Hill and Brockley.

Gail's Bakery

Delicious pastries and bread. They also sell great yoghurt, milk, and butter. Located throughout London, make sure to check where your one is.

Snacks:

In London, there are lots of places to have a quick snack when you are visiting something.

Cuppacha - Chinatown

Excellent bubble tea located in Chinatown in Central London, near Leicester Square.

Bubblewrap - London

Exceptional bubble waffles with a wide range of flavours. Located in Chinatown.

Crêpes à la Carte - Victoria Park

Great crêpes from a food truck in Victoria Park in Hackney, north-east London. There is also a restaurant in Kentish Town and they have a market stall in Camden Market.
