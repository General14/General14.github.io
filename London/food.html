Things to Eat

London is full of so many great things to eat. Here you can find restaurants, cafés, and snacks.

Restaurants:

There are lots of restaurants in London. Here is a list of my favourites.

Monkatsu
Superb sushi and other Japanese food. Located at 49 N Cross Rd, London SE22 9ET.
Mamma Dough
Delicious pizza and other Italian food. There are lots of locations. Here is a list. Also, dogs are allowed in this restaurant.
Voodoo Rays
Tasty New York Style pizza. Located at 285 Rye Ln, London SE15 4UA, and 95 Kingsland High St, London E8 2PB.
Chisou - Mayfair
Sushi in the heart of London, just like being in Japan. On the pricey side but worth it. Located at 22-23 Woodstock St, London W1C 2AR.
Plum Valley
Amazing Cantonese food and dim sum. Located at 20 Gerrard St, London W1D 6JQ.
Wuli Wuli
Excellent Chinese food. Located at 15 Camberwell Church St, London SE5 8TR.
Momo Sushi
Great sushi and other Japanese food. Located at 23 Camberwell Church St, London SE5 8TR.
Silk Road
Authentic Chinese food in south east London. Located at 49 Camberwell Church St, London SE5 8TR.
Nandos
Amazing chicken, with great, delicious sauces. There are many locations, here is a list.
Mr Bao
Really good Taiwanese food and bao. Located at 293 Rye Ln, London SE15 4UA.

Pubs:

Dogs are allowed in most pubs across the UK.

The Herne Tavern
Delectable pub food and great drinks. Located at 2 Forest Hill Rd, London SE22 0RR.
The Lordship Pub
Yummy pub food and good drinks. Located at 211 Lordship Ln, London SE22 8HA.

Cafés:

There are also lots of cafés in London. Here are some of my favourites:

French Café Bistro
Great coffee and other French food. There are also lots of French products here. Located at 44 Forest Hill Rd, London SE22 0RR. Also, dogs are allowed in this café.
St David Coffee House / Bon Dulwich / Browns of Brockley
Really tasty coffee and pastries, as well as great local bread. There are locations in Forest Hill (St David Coffee House, 5 David's Rd, London SE23 3EP), East Dulwich (Bon, 43 Lordship Ln, London SE22 8EW), and Brockley (Browns of Brockley, 5-6 Coulgate St, London SE4 2RW). Also, dogs are allowed in all three locations.
Gail's Bakery
Delicious pastries and bread, good coffee. They also sell great yoghurt, milk, and butter. Here is a list of all the locations. Also, dogs are allowed in this restaurant but only for take-away.
Norris + Knight
Delectable Spanish food and amazing coffee. Located at 118 Forest Hill Rd, London SE22 0RS. Also, dogs are allowed in this café.
Rise and Bloom
Excellent coffee and pastries. Located at 44 Barry Rd, London SE22 0HU. Also, dogs are allowed in this restaurant.

Snacks:

In London, there are lots of places to have a quick snack when you are visiting something.

Cuppacha
Excellent bubble tea in Chinatown. Located at 23 Newport Ct, London WC2H 7JS.
Bubblewrap
Exceptional bubble waffles with a wide range of flavours in Chinatown. Located at 24 Wardour St, London W1D 6QJ.
