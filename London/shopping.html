Shopping

There are so many places to go shopping in London. Some of my favourites are John Lewis, Japan Centre, Canary Wharf Area, and Jones of Brockley.

Japan Centre
Japan Centre is a large Japanese Grocery Store. There you can buy sushi supplies or just some Japanese pickles. Located at 35b Panton St, London SW1Y 4EA.
Jones of Brockley
A great general store with fresh local bread and delicious pastries. Located at East Dulwich (53 Grove Vale, London SE22 8EQ) and Crofton Park (354 Brockley Rd, London SE4 2BY).
John Lewis
John Lewis is a department store with many things. From homeware to kitchenware you can get everything you need. There are many locations. Here is the full list of locations.
Canary Wharf Area
Canary Wharf is a shopping district in East London. There are lots of shops and restaurants so make sure to check on their website.
Harrods
Harrods is the classic London department store, though it is a bit pricey. Located at 87-135 Brompton Rd, London SW1X 7XL.
