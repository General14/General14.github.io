Things to Do

There are so many things to do in London. These are some of my favourites:

Science Museum

A great way to spend a few hours having fun in the world of science. Sometimes they have special exhibitions which you can check out here. Located on Exhibition Road in South Kensington.

Winter Wonderland - Hyde Park

Winter Wonderland is a winter themed fun fair open between late November and Earky January every year. Located in the large Hyde Park located in Central London.
