Things to Do

There are so many things to do in London. My favourites are the Science Museum, Winter Wonderland, Emirates Air Line, DLR, and Kew Gardens.

Science Museum
A great way to spend a few hours having fun in the world of science. Sometimes they have special exhibitions, which you can check out here. Located at Exhibition Rd, South Kensington, London SW7 2DD.
Winter Wonderland - Hyde Park
Winter Wonderland is a winter-themed funfair open between late November and early January every year. Located at Hyde Park, London W2 2UH.
Emirates Air Line
The Emirates Air Line is a cable car that goes from the Greenwich Peninsula to Royal Victoria Dock. It is a great way to see the city. Located at Unit 1, 2, 3 & 4 Emirates Cable Car Terminal, Edmund Halley Way, London SE10 0FR.
DLR
The DLR is a fun way to get around East London. It is also fun for kids to pretend to drive the train as there is no driver. To find out more go to this link. There are many stations, so make sure to check where your nearest one is.
Kew Gardens
Kew Gardens is a large botanical garden located near Richmond. Located at Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew, Richmond TW9 3AE. If you go here make sure to go to Chez Lindsay. If you decide to go here make sure to leave via the Victoria Gate Exit.
London Zoo
The London Zoo is a great way to spend time in Regent's Park. Located at Outer Cir, London NW1 4RY.
