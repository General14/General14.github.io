Things to Do

There are so many things to do in London. These are some of my favourites:

Science Museum

A great way to spend a few hours having fun in the world of science. Sometimes they have special exhibitions which you can check out here. Located at Exhibition Rd, South Kensington, London SW7 2DD.

Winter Wonderland - Hyde Park

Winter Wonderland is a winter-themed funfair open between late November and early January every year. Located at Hyde Park, London W2 2UH.

Emirates Air Line

The Emirates Air Line is a cable car that goes from Greenwich to Royal Victoria Dock. It is a great way to see the city. Located at Unit 1, 2, 3 & 4 Emirates Cable Car Terminal, Edmund Halley Way, London SE10 0FR.

DLR

The DLR is a fun way to get around Eastern London. It is also fun for kids to pretend to drive ithe train as there is no driver. To find out more go to this link. There are many stations, so make sure to check where you nearest one is.
