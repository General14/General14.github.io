Dog-Friendly Things

There are lots of things in London that are dog-friendly. Here you can find a list of dog friendly places in London.

Things to Eat

Restaurants:
Mamma Dough
Delicious pizza and other Italian food. There are lots of locations. Here is a list.
Pubs:
The Herne Tavern
Delectable pub food and great drinks. Located at 2 Forest Hill Rd, London SE22 0RR.
The Lordship Pub
Yummy pub food and good drinks. Located at 211 Lordship Ln, London SE22 8HA.
Cafés:
French Café Bistro
Great coffee and other French food. There are also lots of French products here. Located at 44 Forest Hill Rd, London SE22 0RR.
St David Coffee House / Bon Dulwich / Browns of Brockley
Really tasty coffee and pastries, as well as great local bread. There are locations in Forest Hill (St David Coffee House, 5 David's Rd, London SE23 3EP), East Dulwich (Bon, 43 Lordship Ln, London SE22 8EW), and Brockley (Browns of Brockley, 5-6 Coulgate St, London SE4 2RW).
Norris + Knight
Delectable Spanish food and amazing coffee. Located at 118 Forest Hill Rd, London SE22 0RS.

Things to Do

DLR
The DLR is a fun way to get around East London. It is also fun for kids to pretend to drive the train as there is no driver. To find out more go to this link. There are many stations, so make sure to check where your nearest one is.
Emirates Air Line
The Emirates Air Line is a cable car that goes from the Greenwich Peninsula to Royal Victoria Dock. It is a great way to see the city. Located at Unit 1, 2, 3 & 4 Emirates Cable Car Terminal, Edmund Halley Way, London SE10 0FR.
Royal Parks
The Royal Parks are a group of parks in Central London. If you need something to do then take your dog on a walk here. Located at Hyde Park, Regent's Park, and St James's Park.

Shopping

John Lewis
John Lewis is a department store with many things. From homeware to kitchenware you can get everything you need. There are many locations. Here is the full list of locations.
Canary Wharf Area
Canary Wharf is a shopping district in East London. There are lots of shops and restaurants so make sure to check on their website. Some allow dogs but some don't so make sure to check.
Jones of Brockley
A great general store with fresh local bread and delicious pastries. Located in East Dulwich (53 Grove Vale, London SE22 8EQ) and Crofton Park (354 Brockley Rd, London SE4 2BY).
