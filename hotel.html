Places to Stay

Here are some nice hotels that I know, I hope you like them

  • Rockwell East - Apartments

    This hotel is very modern. You have you own apartment with amenities, it is very nice.

    It is near Tower Gateway, near Tower Bridge, on the DLR. This is near Tower Hill and this is on the Circle & District line you can take this from London Victoria.



  • The Ned

    This hotel is in Central London in a Grade I listed building. It is very fancy and very nice.

    It is near Bank, a very large underground station, you can get here from London Bridge and many other places.



  • Tower Suites - By Blue Orchid

    This hotel is very modern and very nice.

    Similar to Rockwell East, it is near Tower Gateway, near Tower Bridge, on the DLR. This is near Tower Hill and this is on the Circle & District line you can take this from London Victoria.



  • Clayton Hotel

    A very modern and lovely hotel.

    it is near Aldgate East on the Hammersmith and City & District lines, you can take these to London St Pancras, and London Victoria.
